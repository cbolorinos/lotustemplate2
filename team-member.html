Dr. Scott Riley's passion is helping people to enhance their own natural looks, a passion he pursues as one of the leading plastic surgeons in the San Diego area. Specializing in plastic surgery as well as in cosmetic and reconstructive breast surgery and body contouring procedures, Dr. Riley spearheaded the establishment of a regional plastic surgery practice that serves our city and the surrounding area with excellence and efficiency.

His impressive medical education and advanced surgical training established the foundation of his successful plastic surgery practice. A San Francisco native, Dr. Riley completed his undergraduate science degree at San Diego's University after moving here in 1985. He earned his medical degree from the University of Los Angeles, where he also completed his plastic surgery residency. He is highly valued in the community of plastic and reconstructive surgeons as an expert with extensive qualification.
